Emergency First Aid Instructions

❤️ Heart Attack

Keep patient calm. Loosen tight clothes. Call emergency immediately.

🧠 Stroke

Check FAST: Face drooping, Arm weakness, Speech difficulty.

🩸 Heavy Bleeding

Apply firm pressure using clean cloth. Elevate injured area.

🔥 Burns

Cool burn under running water for 10 minutes. Do not apply oil.
